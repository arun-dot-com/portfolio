Patent

A System to Detect and Notify Users about Intrusion in a Farmland

  • Published a utility patent for an innovative hardware-software solution designed to enhance farm security by detecting animal intrusions. This system integrates a NoIR camera with a Raspberry Pi and Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) technology to monitor farmlands in real-time.
  • The NoIR camera captures images under low-light conditions, ensuring continuous surveillance, day or night. The Raspberry Pi serves as the processing unit, running the CNN model trained to identify various animal species that may pose a threat to crops. This combination allows for efficient processing and analysis of visual data.
  • By providing immediate notifications of animal presence, this system helps farmers take proactive measures to protect their crops from potential damage. The solution not only aims to reduce crop loss but also minimizes the need for harmful deterrents, promoting sustainable farming practices.
  • This patent holds significant potential for application in smart farming technologies, enhancing existing agricultural monitoring systems and contributing to the advancement of precision agriculture.
